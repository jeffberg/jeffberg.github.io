About

Where Poetry Meets Data

I see the world through two lenses: the poetic and the empirical. As a doctoral researcher at New York University, I study the intricate systems that shape how billions of people discover, consume, and engage with digital media. My work sits at the intersection of behavioral science, large-scale data analytics, and platform intelligence.

"The best data tells a story. The best stories are built on truth. I chase both."

— J. Berg

The Poet

Language shapes thought. I bring a poet's sensitivity to pattern, rhythm, and meaning into my research—finding the human narrative beneath the numbers, and communicating complex ideas with clarity and resonance.

The Scientist

Rigor drives discovery. I apply deep learning architectures, session-level behavioral modeling, and causal inference methods to understand engagement at platform scale—from YouTube recommendation systems to digital media ecosystems at large.

Expertise

What I Do

YouTube Engagement Analytics

Session-level modeling of user behavior on the world's largest video platform. Understanding watch time, satisfaction signals, and the dynamics of recommendation-driven consumption.

Deep Learning & AI

Applying deep neural network architectures to behavioral prediction, content understanding, and large-scale media intelligence problems in the spirit of Google DeepMind research.

Digital Media Intelligence

Analyzing platform ecosystems, algorithmic amplification, and audience dynamics to inform responsible media strategy, policy, and academic understanding of the digital public sphere.
